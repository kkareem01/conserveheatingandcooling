AC Installation Questions

AC installation FAQ

How long does an AC installation take?

Most residential installs are completed in one day. Larger or more complex jobs (multi-system, ductwork modifications, attic-handler relocations) can take 1–2 days. We give you a clear timeline before we start.

How much does a new AC system cost in Grayson?

It depends on home size, efficiency tier (SEER2 rating), and brand. We do a free in-home estimate so the price reflects your home's actual needs — not a generic ballpark.

What size AC do I need?

A real Manual J load calculation tells us — not a rule of thumb based on square footage alone. Insulation, windows, sun exposure, ceiling height, and ductwork all matter. Oversized systems short-cycle, undersized ones run constantly. We size it right.

Do you offer financing?

Yes — we partner with HVAC-specific lenders offering promotional financing on qualifying installs. Ask during your estimate and we'll walk through the options.

Which brand should I choose?

Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Goodman, and Rheem all make great equipment at different price points. We'll recommend based on your priorities (efficiency, warranty length, budget) and never push a brand we wouldn't put in our own home.
