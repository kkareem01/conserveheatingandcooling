Installation Questions

New system installation FAQ

How much does a new HVAC system cost in Grayson?

It depends on the size of your home and the efficiency level you choose — which is exactly why we do a proper load calculation instead of guessing. After a quick assessment, we give you a clear written price with no hidden fees.

What size HVAC system do I need?

The right size comes from a load calculation that accounts for your home's square footage, insulation, windows, and layout — not a rule of thumb. Bigger is not better; a correctly sized system is more comfortable and lasts longer.

How long does a new system installation take?

Most residential installations are completed in one day. Larger projects or those involving ductwork can take longer — you'll get a clear timeline with your written quote.

When is the best time to replace my HVAC system?

Before it fails completely. If your system is over 10–15 years old, needs frequent repairs, or your energy bills keep rising, planning a replacement on your schedule beats an emergency in July or January.

Do you offer a warranty on new installations?

Yes. New systems come with a manufacturer's equipment warranty, and Conserve backs every installation with our own written workmanship warranty.
